Yellow Lantern Superman

This is yellow lantern superman inspired by injustice comic series. Superman turns evil and just when he is about to lose from Batman and his allies, Sinestro offers him a yellow ring. This ring is fuelled by anger and Clark Kent has a lot inside since he just lost pregnant Lois to the joker. Actually joker made superman kill Lois with his own hands after exposing him to fear toxic with kryptonite.

Difficulties:
